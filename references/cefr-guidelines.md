# CEFR French Vocabulary Guidelines

## Level Characteristics

### A1 — Beginner
- Basic greetings, numbers, colors, days, family members
- Common verbs: être, avoir, faire, aller, aimer, habiter, travailler
- Simple nouns: maison, travail, ami, ville, rue
- Function words: le/la/les, un/une, à, de, dans, sur, et, ou, mais
- **DO NOT extract A1 words for vocabulary list** — they are too basic

### A2 — Elementary
- Extended daily life: quartier, études, cinéma, jardin, boulot
- Common adjectives: beau/belle, grand, petit, difficile, facile, sympa
- Simple compound expressions: il y a, parce que, à côté de
- Descriptive vocabulary: lumière, saison, bâtiment, hauteur
- **Vocabulary starts here** — extract words at A2 and above

### B1 — Intermediate
- Abstract concepts: culturel, densité, exceptionnel, proximité
- Colloquialisms: galérer, boulot, coincé, rigolo, bouffer
- Discourse markers: du coup, en fait, par contre, quand même
- Emotion & opinion: inspirant, rêver, attaché, envie
- Professional: entrepreneurs, vocation, réseau, digital

### B2 — Upper Intermediate
- Nuanced adjectives: alémanique, bourgeois, provincial
- Specialized nouns: ministère, émission, banlieue, point de chute
- Idiomatic expressions: dans le sang, auberge espagnole
- Complex connectors: en revanche, néanmoins, toutefois
- Abstract: évolution, contrainte, ambivalence

### C1 — Advanced
- Literary/rhetorical vocabulary
- Rare or specialized terms
- Nuanced register shifts
- Sophisticated abstractions

## Vocabulary Selection Rules

**默认自动填充 B1 及以上，不要问用户选级别。**

1. **只收 B1 及以上**的词（排除 A1/A2 太基础的词，那些不值得进词汇本）
2. Exclude: pronouns, articles, basic prepositions (à, de, dans, sur), common numbers
3. Exclude: proper nouns (Paris, France, Bordeaux, etc.) unless culturally significant
4. Prefer: content words (nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs)
5. Target: ALL remaining B1+ words that are level-appropriate（不设上限，能收多少收多少）
6. For each word, pick the best example sentence from the transcript
7. Write a concise Chinese definition (~2-10 characters)
